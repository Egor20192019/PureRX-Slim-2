What supplements best help to lose weight?

Supplements for weight loss are one of the most commonly purchased means without a prescription. Some choose them consciously as a supplement to the treatment, leading to weight loss, others treat them as magic pills that will burn the accumulated fat without additional renunciations. However, do not trust the suggestive ads and do not let yourself be fooled by the shapely silhouettes on the packaging. Without a balanced, healthy diet and exercise, we will not achieve the desired effect. Supplements can only be used as an aid and this is what today's article will be about.

Fat burning supplements

Supplements for weight loss can be divided into several categories. Among the most popular are undoubtedly those fat-burning e.g. piperinox. However, remember that even when using this type of preparation, this process occurs only in combination with physical exertion. If we turn away from the movement and do not intend to introduce it into our daily activities, the adoption of such measures misses the point.

Appetite suppressant slimming supplements

Appetite suppressants are recommended for people who find it difficult to give up bad eating habits. Their regular use reduces appetite and allows you to get rid of the need for excessive eating. However, let us not forget that the desired result will be achieved only if we follow a healthy diet and take a reduced number of calories. A qualified nutritionist can help us to plan a suitable menu.

Appetite suppressants usually contain compounds such as caffeine, dietary fibre, guarana, chromium, tyrosine or glucomannan. Examples include: Be slim 3, chitosan, apetiblock, glucomannan, Get slim morning & night, Olimp green coffee premium, white mulberry plus fasolamina, appetite stop and bio-chromium.

Fat-blocking supplements

This type of dietary supplements for weight loss is able to block the absorption of even 1/3 of the fats consumed. In combination with a light diet and regular exercise, it helps to lose excess pounds, effectively accelerating the process of weight reduction. Taking them according to the guidelines, we also do not expose ourselves to side effects, since these preparations work only within the digestive tract.

The most popular fat-blocking supplements include: Alli, Calominal, Xenical, Chitinin Extra, Chitobon and Redufat

Supplements for weight loss-is it worth using them?

Supplements for weight loss can help reduce body fat. However, they will never replace a healthy diet and adequate dose of exercise, which are often enough to lose excess pounds. If we do not have a strong will, we can include them in the treatment to speed up its course. However, let us remember that they must be part of the change of daily habits, and not a means on which we base the process of weight loss.
