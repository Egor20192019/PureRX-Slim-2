About us for you

Once I did not take care of myself (I ate junk food, did not go in for sports and "had fun" in every possible way) until I let myself go to such an extent that it became scary to look in the mirror. I decided to" pull myself together " and change, as in the various stories that the Internet is full of. That I didn't try, but it didn't make sense. I realized that information about healthy eating and exercise is not as readily available as we think. After a while I still found the information that became useful to me. Having changed, I decided to help many people like me. I run this blog for free and for my pleasure. I hope I can help people like me. Enjoy reading!
